Info/Help

To add a student, fill out the form with the student's name, year/type, and final grade. Click the "Add/Update Student" button to add the student to the gradebook. If a student already exists with the same name, their grade and class year will be updated.

To delete a student, simply press the delete button in the student's row in the table.
